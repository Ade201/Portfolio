Reprizz

A civic transparency web app that lets users search and explore U.S. politicians, view profiles, and lay the foundation for accountability based on real legislative data.

Overview

Reprizz is an MVP web application built to make political information more accessible. Users can browse and search for politicians, view individual profile pages, and explore a clean, scalable foundation designed for future data expansion such as voting records and district-based search.

Problem

Political data is often fragmented, difficult to navigate, or buried behind complex interfaces. Many people want to quickly understand who represents them and what those representatives are doing, without digging through multiple sources.

Solution

Reprizz provides a simple, fast interface for discovering politicians through live search and profile pages. The architecture is designed to integrate trusted legislative data sources while remaining flexible enough to support future features like ZIP-code search, voting summaries, and funding transparency.
